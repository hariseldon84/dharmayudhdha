# Mahabharata Research Dossier

Goal: Aggregate rare, conflicting, and suppressed narratives; primary sources; philological notes; regional retellings; scholarly papers; archaeology; epigraphy; and living oral traditions that inform Book 1 — The Parshurama Prophecy. This master list prioritizes credible, citable references. Each item includes a short note highlighting the “startling” or under-discussed angle where relevant.

Note on sourcing ethics: Where possible, we link to authoritative editions, archives, universities, and libraries. Many primary texts are under copyright or behind paywalls; links here point to catalogs, abstracts, or legitimate previews. Use library access or institutional subscriptions for full texts.
